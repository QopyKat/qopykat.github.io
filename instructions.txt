Travel Planning Task: Tokyo Trip

Goal
- Create a detailed travel plan for a Tokyo trip for 2 people.
- Trip length: 2 to 3 weeks.
- Timeframe: December, possibly including Christmas.
- Exact travel dates are not known yet.

High-Level Scope
- Focus mostly on Tokyo.
- Also include single-day excursions to other areas.
- Also research whether a 1-night trip to a nearby hot spring town is a good option.
- Do not research Tokyo hotel options; accommodation in Tokyo will likely be an Airbnb.

Traveler Preferences
- Do not focus on stereotypical tourist attractions such as Tokyo Tower.
- Preferred travel style / interests:
  - food
  - nightlife (but not discos / clubbing)
  - neighborhoods
  - wandering / walking around
  - nature
  - anime
  - architecture
  - games
  - culture
- Create a mix of:
  - relaxing / lighter days
  - packed / busy days
  - indoor days for very cold / snowy / rainy weather
  - outdoor snowy-day plans where snow would be especially beautiful

Research Requirements
- Look for ideas online using multiple source types, including:
  - reviews
  - “top XXX” lists
  - recommendation articles / guides
  - other useful recommendation sources
- For each activity in each day plan, do extensive research.
- Include direct links wherever relevant, for example:
  - official websites
  - booking pages
  - attraction information pages
  - hike route pages
  - restaurant pages
  - maps
- For attractions, restaurants, and other planned stops, research opening times and account for them in the plans.
- Research important practical information and include it in the documentation, for example:
  - SIM card / eSIM options
  - train tickets / rail passes / transport cards
  - whether cash is needed vs. card acceptance
  - where to get cash if needed
- Investigate whether it is actually true that shops are closed for 2 days over Christmas.
- Do not assume this; verify it.
- If Christmas closures limit options, research alternatives such as hikes or other suitable activities.
- Research whether some kind of 2-week / 3-week / 1-month public transport pass would make sense, considering public transport costs.
- Research any “special” restaurants that are especially worth checking out.

Hot Spring Town Research
- Check whether a 1-night trip to a hot spring town that is not too far from Tokyo is feasible and worthwhile.
- Provide several options.
- Include:
  - hotel / ryokan prices
  - reviews
  - traditional Japanese inns
  - options that may include a private / personal onsen

Day Plan Requirements
- Create about 30 to 40 day plans total so there are plenty of options to choose from.
- Dates do not need to be fixed.
- If a specific event only happens on certain dates or days, it may be tied to those dates.
- Create special day plans for special dates / occasions such as:
  - Christmas Eve
  - New Year’s
  - other notable holiday-period days if relevant
- Avoid repeating very similar neighborhoods or experiences too often across the set of day plans.
- Each recommendation should be tagged as one of:
  - strong fit
  - experimental
  - weather-dependent

Deliverables
- Put everything in one folder.
- Create one markdown file per day plan.
- Also create one general markdown file that gives an overview of all day plans.
- If there are small miscellaneous recommendations that could fit into almost any day plan, include them in the general markdown file.

Required Content in Each Day Plan File
- Short summary at the top.
- Estimated cost.
- Estimated transit time.
- Map links.
- Well-researched activities with relevant links.
- Opening-time considerations where relevant.

Required Content in the General Overview File
- Overview of all day plans.
- Top 10 must-do day plans, prioritized / ranked.
- Miscellaneous recommendations that fit flexibly into multiple plans.
- Important practical trip information gathered during research.

Quality / Process Rules
- Do not make uncertain assumptions.
- If something important is unclear or uncertain, ask me in between.
- After finishing, review everything again and check whether there are any additional relevant considerations for each point / recommendation.
