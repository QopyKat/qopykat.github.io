Jimbocho, Ochanomizu, and Kanda Day

Used bookstores, music shops, curry, shrine culture, and low-key old-Tokyo urban texture.

Photo highlights

Suggested flow

  • Start in Jimbocho Book Town for used books, coffee, and curry.
  • Walk north through Ochanomizu instrument streets.
  • Continue to Kanda Myojin for a culture/anime crossover stop.
  • If energy is still high, finish with a short Akihabara detour without making it the whole day.

Opening-time considerations

  • Bookstores and specialty shops often open late morning, so this is not a dawn-start day.
  • Shrine visit is flexible; retail browsing is the timing-sensitive part.

Why this is a fit

  • Excellent for games/anime-adjacent culture without spending the whole day in loud arcades.

Extra planning notes

  • Best for: city days where you want one clear anchor plus plenty of room for food, coffee, or unplanned browsing.
  • Pacing tip: this plan works well if you keep one major stop fixed and treat the rest as optional rather than trying to maximize every block.
  • Easy adjustment: if energy drops or the weather shifts, shorten the walking portion and turn the day into one anchor activity plus a better dinner or cafe finish.
