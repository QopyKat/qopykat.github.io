Harajuku hololive and Shibuya IP Day

A VTuber-leaning day built around the current hololive Harajuku shop, Shibuya anime pop-ups, and flexible merch hunting.

Photo highlights

Suggested flow

  • Start at the hololive production official shop in Harajuku, which is currently operating at OMOKADO in Harajuku.
  • Walk down toward Shibuya rather than taking a train if the weather is good; this keeps the day from becoming only store-hopping.
  • Use Tokyo Anime Center in Shibuya as the event anchor if the current exhibition fits your tastes.
  • Finish at SHIBUYA TSUTAYA, where the IP SHOTEN floor explicitly covers comic, anime, and VTuber content.

Opening-time considerations

  • The current hololive Harajuku shop lists 11:00 to 20:00 and warns that numbered tickets or entry restrictions may be used on busy days.
  • Tokyo Anime Center's Shibuya venue info lists 11:00 to 19:00, but exhibition hours can vary.
  • SHIBUYA TSUTAYA floor guide lists IP SHOTEN as 10:00 to 21:00.

Why this is a fit

  • Best dedicated VTuber-oriented day in the set.
  • Easy to scale up or down depending on whether the current pop-ups are worth your time.
  • Strong bad-weather option because almost all of it is indoors.

Extra planning notes

  • Best for: fandom-heavy days where shopping, pop-ups, and current collabs are a real part of the fun rather than an afterthought.
  • Pacing tip: set a budget ceiling early, because these days are easy to extend by hours once you start finding stores or limited goods you like.
  • Easy adjustment: if the current event or merch situation looks weak, keep the area but pivot toward cafes, arcades, or one neighboring district instead.
