# 21. Mt. Takao Nature Day

Short summary: The easiest satisfying nature day from Tokyo, especially good in crisp winter weather.  
Tag: `hiking`

- Estimated total cost per person: `JPY 2,000 to 5,000`
- Cost breakdown:
  - Transit per person: `JPY 800 to 1,600`
  - Food, snacks, and other: `JPY 1,200 to 3,400`
- Estimated transit time from central Tokyo base: `60 to 90 min each way`
- Transit breakdown:
  - Mode mix: mostly `train + mountain walking`, with optional `cable car/lift`
  - Rough route: travel `far west` out of central Tokyo toward `Takaosanguchi`, then switch from rail travel to the mountain approach and trail network
- Map links:
  - [Mt. Takao](https://maps.google.com/?q=Mount+Takao)
  - [Takaosan Yakuoin](https://maps.google.com/?q=Takaosan+Yakuoin)

Suggested flow:
- Use the [official Mt. Takao guide](https://www.takaotozan.co.jp/course/) to choose the route and cable-car decision.
- Hike or partially hike depending on weather and energy.
- Visit [Takaosan Yakuoin](https://www.takaosan.or.jp/) on the mountain route.
- Keep dinner simple back in Tokyo.

Opening-time considerations:
- Start earlier than city days, especially in winter when daylight is shorter.
- Check wind, visibility, and any snow/ice warnings before committing.

Why this is a fit:
- Best easy-access nature day from Tokyo.

## Extra planning notes

- Best for: using a good weather window well and committing to one regional area instead of trying to combine too many side trips.
- Pacing tip: start earlier than on Tokyo city days and keep at least one part of the itinerary skippable in case transit, weather, or crowds slow you down.
- Easy adjustment: if conditions look poor in the morning, replace this with one of the indoor-heavy Tokyo plans and save the trip for a clearer day.
