# 34. Ikebukuro Collector and Event Day

Short summary: A deeper Ikebukuro fandom day aimed at people who want more than one quick Animate stop.  
Tag: `strong fit`

- Estimated total cost per person: `JPY 3,000 to 8,000` before shopping
- Cost breakdown:
  - Transit per person: `JPY 400 to 800`
  - Food, cafes, and other: `JPY 2,600 to 7,200` before shopping
- Estimated transit time from central Tokyo base: `20 to 35 min each way`
- Transit breakdown:
  - Mode mix: mostly `JR/subway + walking`
  - Rough route: travel `northwest` to `Ikebukuro`, stay concentrated between `Animate`, `Otome Road`, and the `K-BOOKS` cluster, and avoid unnecessary station recrossing
- Map links:
  - [animate Ikebukuro Flagship Store](https://maps.google.com/?q=animate+Ikebukuro+Flagship+Store)
  - [Otome Road area](https://maps.google.com/?q=Otome+Road+Ikebukuro)
  - [K-BOOKS Ikebukuro area](https://maps.google.com/?q=Higashi-Ikebukuro)

Suggested flow:
- Start at [animate Ikebukuro Flagship Store](https://www.animate.co.jp/en/shop/ikebukuro/), then check whether [Space Galleria](https://www.animate.co.jp/en/spacegalleria/ikebukuro/) or another in-building event is worth committing to.
- Use the wider [K-BOOKS Ikebukuro store network](https://www.k-books.co.jp/en/) rather than just one store; there are separate game, character, live, and oshikatsu-oriented branches.
- Walk the Otome Road side streets so the day is not only vertical retail.
- Keep a cafe or early dinner break in the middle, because this is one of the easier “accidental six hours of merch browsing” days in the set.

Opening-time considerations:
- Animate Ikebukuro lists `10:00 to 21:00`.
- Many K-BOOKS branches open later, often around `12:00` on weekdays and `11:30` on weekends/holidays.
- Best structure is Animate first, K-BOOKS cluster second, dinner third.

Why this is a fit:
- Better than the standard Ikebukuro day if you are specifically into goods, collabs, resale hunting, or current anime/game fandom culture.
- Especially useful for titles that are underrepresented in more tourist-facing official stores.

## Extra planning notes

- Best for: fandom-heavy days where shopping, pop-ups, and current collabs are a real part of the fun rather than an afterthought.
- Pacing tip: set a budget ceiling early, because these days are easy to extend by hours once you start finding stores or limited goods you like.
- Easy adjustment: if the current event or merch situation looks weak, keep the area but pivot toward cafes, arcades, or one neighboring district instead.
