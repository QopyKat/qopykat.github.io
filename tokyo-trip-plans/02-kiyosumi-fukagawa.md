# 02. Kiyosumi-Shirakawa and Fukagawa Day

Short summary: Coffee, canals, gardens, and understated east-side culture with less tourist friction.  
Tag: `strong fit`

- Estimated total cost per person: `JPY 2,500 to 6,500`
- Cost breakdown:
  - Transit per person: `JPY 400 to 800`
  - Food, coffee, admissions, and other: `JPY 2,100 to 5,700`
- Estimated transit time from central Tokyo base: `20 to 30 min each way`
- Transit breakdown:
  - Mode mix: mostly `subway + flat walking`
  - Rough route: go `east` toward `Kiyosumi-Shirakawa`, walk between cafes, garden, and museum areas, then either return directly or make one short subway hop inside Koto City
- Map links:
  - [Kiyosumi Garden](https://maps.google.com/?q=Kiyosumi+Garden)
  - [Museum of Contemporary Art Tokyo](https://maps.google.com/?q=Museum+of+Contemporary+Art+Tokyo)

Suggested flow:
- Begin with a cafe crawl in Kiyosumi-Shirakawa using the neighborhood overview from [GO TOKYO](https://www.gotokyo.org/en/destinations/eastern-tokyo/kiyosumi-shirakawa/index.html).
- Walk through [Kiyosumi Garden](https://www.tokyo-park.or.jp/park/kiyosumi/index.html).
- Choose one cultural anchor:
  - [Museum of Contemporary Art Tokyo](https://www.mot-art-museum.jp/en/)
  - [Fukagawa Edo Museum](https://www.kcf.or.jp/fukagawa/)
- End with an evening canal or river walk before dinner.

Opening-time considerations:
- Kiyosumi Garden is daytime-oriented.
- The Museum of Contemporary Art Tokyo is usually a `10:00 to 18:00` museum day; do it before dinner, not after.
- Fukagawa Edo Museum is a better pick if you want something compact and less mentally heavy than a full art museum.

Why this is a fit:
- One of the best non-stereotypical Tokyo days.
- Excellent for architecture, calm walking, and coffee.

## Extra planning notes

- Best for: city days where you want one clear anchor plus plenty of room for food, coffee, or unplanned browsing.
- Pacing tip: this plan works well if you keep one major stop fixed and treat the rest as optional rather than trying to maximize every block.
- Easy adjustment: if energy drops or the weather shifts, shorten the walking portion and turn the day into one anchor activity plus a better dinner or cafe finish.
