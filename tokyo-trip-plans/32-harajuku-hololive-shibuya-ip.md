# 32. Harajuku hololive and Shibuya IP Day

Short summary: A VTuber-leaning day built around the current hololive Harajuku shop, Shibuya anime pop-ups, and flexible merch hunting.  
Tag: `experimental`

- Estimated total cost per person: `JPY 3,000 to 8,000` before merch shopping
- Cost breakdown:
  - Transit per person: `JPY 350 to 700`
  - Food, event tickets if any, and other: `JPY 2,650 to 7,300` before merch shopping
- Estimated transit time from central Tokyo base: `15 to 30 min each way`
- Transit breakdown:
  - Mode mix: mostly `train/subway + downhill city walking`
  - Rough route: go `west` to `Harajuku`, then walk south toward `Shibuya` if weather is good, keeping the route on foot between the main fandom stops
- Map links:
  - [hololive production official shop in Harajuku](https://maps.google.com/?q=Tokyu+Plaza+Omotesando+Omokado)
  - [Tokyo Anime Center Shibuya](https://maps.google.com/?q=Tokyo+Anime+Center+Shibuya)
  - [SHIBUYA TSUTAYA](https://maps.google.com/?q=SHIBUYA+TSUTAYA)

Suggested flow:
- Start at the [hololive production official shop in Harajuku](https://hololive.hololivepro.com/en/events/shopharajuku/), which is currently operating at OMOKADO in Harajuku.
- Walk down toward Shibuya rather than taking a train if the weather is good; this keeps the day from becoming only store-hopping.
- Use [Tokyo Anime Center in Shibuya](https://tokyoanimecenter.jp/en/) as the event anchor if the current exhibition fits your tastes.
- Finish at [SHIBUYA TSUTAYA](https://shibuyatsutaya.tsite.jp/index.html), where the [IP SHOTEN floor](https://shibuyatsutaya.tsite.jp/ip-shoten/) explicitly covers comic, anime, and VTuber content.

Opening-time considerations:
- The current hololive Harajuku shop lists `11:00 to 20:00` and warns that numbered tickets or entry restrictions may be used on busy days.
- [Tokyo Anime Center's Shibuya venue info](https://tokyoanimecenter.jp/en/about/shibuya/) lists `11:00 to 19:00`, but exhibition hours can vary.
- [SHIBUYA TSUTAYA floor guide](https://shibuyatsutaya.tsite.jp/floor/) lists `IP SHOTEN` as `10:00 to 21:00`.

Why this is a fit:
- Best dedicated VTuber-oriented day in the set.
- Easy to scale up or down depending on whether the current pop-ups are worth your time.
- Strong bad-weather option because almost all of it is indoors.

## Extra planning notes

- Best for: fandom-heavy days where shopping, pop-ups, and current collabs are a real part of the fun rather than an afterthought.
- Pacing tip: set a budget ceiling early, because these days are easy to extend by hours once you start finding stores or limited goods you like.
- Easy adjustment: if the current event or merch situation looks weak, keep the area but pivot toward cafes, arcades, or one neighboring district instead.
