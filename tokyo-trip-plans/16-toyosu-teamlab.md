# 16. Toyosu Marketside and teamLab Day

Short summary: One flashy immersive stop balanced by seafood, waterfront walking, and a more modern Tokyo mood.  
Tag: `strong fit`

- Estimated total cost per person: `JPY 4,000 to 10,000`
- Cost breakdown:
  - Transit per person: `JPY 500 to 1,000`
  - Food, tickets, and other: `JPY 3,500 to 9,000`
- Estimated transit time from central Tokyo base: `20 to 35 min each way`
- Transit breakdown:
  - Mode mix: mostly `subway/train + waterfront walking`
  - Rough route: head `southeast/east` toward `Toyosu`, anchor on the booked `teamLab` slot, then walk between `Toyosu` market-side stops rather than doing many separate train hops
- Map links:
  - [teamLab Planets TOKYO](https://maps.google.com/?q=teamLab+Planets+TOKYO)
  - [Toyosu Senkyaku Banrai](https://maps.google.com/?q=Toyosu+Senkyaku+Banrai)

Suggested flow:
- Reserve an entry slot at [teamLab Planets TOKYO](https://www.teamlab.art/e/planets/).
- Pair it with seafood or snack-hopping at [Toyosu Senkyaku Banrai](https://www.toyosu-senkyakubanrai.jp/languages/en/) rather than trying to overdo the wholesale-market side.
- Add a waterfront walk if the weather is clear.

Opening-time considerations:
- teamLab should be booked in advance, especially for weekends and holiday periods.
- This day is easiest if the ticket time is fixed first and meals are arranged around it.

Why this is a fit:
- One of the best “busy but easy” Tokyo days.

## Extra planning notes

- Best for: city days where you want one clear anchor plus plenty of room for food, coffee, or unplanned browsing.
- Pacing tip: this plan works well if you keep one major stop fixed and treat the rest as optional rather than trying to maximize every block.
- Easy adjustment: if energy drops or the weather shifts, shorten the walking portion and turn the day into one anchor activity plus a better dinner or cafe finish.
